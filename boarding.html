Dog Boarding

Our spacious indoor kennels, built to the most recent design and specifications, each have private sleeping quarters, ensuring your dog's comfort and safety at all times. Dogs from the same household can be accommodated together at the owners' request.


Contact Us

24 hr Presence


There is a physical presence on the property 24 hours a day, including CCTV and guard dogs.

Our kennels also have smoke and carbon monoxide alarms.


Our Kennels


All of our kennels are heated and well-insulated log structures that provide warm, comfortable accommodations for dogs of all breeds, ages, and sizes.

In addition to our indoor kennels, the dogs have access to a large outside weatherproofed covered run during the day.

Four times a day, supervised exercise is provided within secure fenced exercise areas


Pricing
