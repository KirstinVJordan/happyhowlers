Boarding

grooming


We provide a caring and friendly service and walk customers through the various options for their specific breed of dog. The best way to determine the most appropriate maintenance plan is to meet with one of the groomers and discuss your pet's specific needs.


Training


Positive reinforcement is a communication method that we use to increase the behaviours you want to see more of. This is a fun "game" for your dog, to make sure they are enthusiastic about participating in their learning.
